Solutions & Action Plan

Practical ideas to improve water in both communities

Good News: Both communities show high willingness to participate (78-82%), so solutions have strong community support!

For Soweto

  • SMS Alert System: Text people about water cuts
  • Community Notice Boards: Post updates in public places
  • Meter Repair Teams: Fix broken water meters quickly
  • Water Saving Workshops: Teach practical tips
Time to Implement 3-6 months

For Alexandra

  • Emergency Drain Repair: Fix flooding immediately
  • Sewer Cleaning Program: Clear blockages urgently
  • Community Clean-up: Reduce litter in drains
  • Basic Plumbing Training: Teach locals to fix small issues
Time to Implement 1-3 months (urgent)
